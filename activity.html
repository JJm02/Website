Activities in Labuan Bird Park

🦚 Peacock Feeding

Time: 9:00 AM – 4:30 PM

Visitors of all ages will enjoy this all-day feeding session featuring our most interactive animals. Hand-feed ducks waddling through the park, spot elegant peacocks showing off their feathers, pet fluffy rabbits, or watch fish swim up for treats. Perfect for kids and families!

🕊️ Stork Feeding

Time: 2:30 PM – 3:00 PM

Get up close to our tall, graceful storks during their afternoon feeding time. This is a calm and fascinating session where you can observe their slow movements and majestic presence as they feed.

🦜 Hornbill Feeding

Time: 3:00 PM – 3:30 PM

Don’t miss the hornbills — one of Borneo’s most iconic birds! Watch as these intelligent and striking birds feed using their powerful beaks. It's a rare chance to witness their unique behavior up close.

🌈 Sun Conure Feeding

Time: 3:30 PM – 4:00 PM

These bright, colorful parrots are full of energy! See them chatter, play, and flutter around as they enjoy their snacks. A joyful and photogenic moment that’s sure to brighten your day.

These are just a few of the many exciting experiences waiting for you at Labuan Bird Park! From learning about exotic species to relaxing walks surrounded by nature, there's always something special happening here. We invite you to come and discover even more fun activities in person — plan your visit today and let the adventure begin!
