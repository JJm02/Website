Bird Gallery in Labuan Bird Park

Hornbill

The Sarawak Hornbill, also known as the Rhinoceros Hornbill, is an iconic bird of Borneo. It features a striking curved bill and a bright orange casque on top of its beak. Revered in local culture, this majestic bird symbolizes strength and is often spotted in dense tropical rainforests.

Peacock

This blue and green-colored Indian Peacock is famous for its iridescent plumage and dramatic tail feathers, which it fans out in a courtship display. Often found wandering freely in the park, its vibrant colors make it one of the most photogenic birds in the sanctuary.

Eagle

This eagle species features a bold white head and a rich brown body. Known for its sharp eyesight and powerful build, it is a top predator in the sky. Commonly referred to as the White-headed Sea Eagle, it is a symbol of freedom and strength.

Parrot

This white-colored parrot with a yellow crest is known as the Sulphur-crested Cockatoo. Native to Australia and parts of Southeast Asia, it is a social and intelligent bird, often mimicking human voices and displaying playful behavior.

These are just a few of the beautiful bird species you can discover at Labuan Bird Park. With even more colorful, rare, and majestic birds waiting to be seen, your visit will be filled with wonder and excitement. Come explore the full variety in person and experience the beauty of nature up close!
